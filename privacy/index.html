Privacy Policy

Effective July 20, 2026 · Built by Josh Studio LLC, d/b/a Tynkr Tools & Co

The short version: the apps collect nothing, this website collects standard analytics, and your email only exists on Substack if you chose to give it to them. Here is the whole story.

The Tynkr apps collect nothing

Tynkr Ledger, Vault, Hearth, and Forge are local-first desktop apps. There are no accounts, no telemetry, no analytics, and no cloud. Everything you enter is stored in files on your own computer, and we have no way to see, receive, or recover any of it. The only network activity an app performs is:

  • An optional update check — a download of a static version file and installer from our public release feed on GitHub. Nothing about you or your data is sent, and you can turn the check off in Settings.
  • License validation (only on copies activated with a license key) — transmits the license key and a random installation identifier, and nothing else. Never your files, your content, or your personal information.

If you place an app's data folder or backups inside a synced folder (OneDrive, Dropbox, Google Drive), that copy is governed by that provider's terms — the choice, and the folder, are yours.

What this website collects

AnalyticsThis site uses Google Analytics 4 to understand aggregate traffic — pages visited, approximate region, device type. Google sets cookies to do this and processes the data under Google's privacy policy. We see aggregate reports, never a profile of you. The site works fine with an ad blocker or with cookies disabled — we won't nag you about it.
HostingThe site is served by GitHub Pages. GitHub may log standard web-server request data (like IP addresses) per the GitHub Privacy Statement.
FontsPages load typefaces from Google Fonts, which means your browser requests font files from Google's servers.

That's it. The site has no accounts, no comment system, and no forms that submit to us.

The email list lives on Substack

If you join the Tynkr list, the signup happens on Substack — this site just sends you there. Your email address is stored and processed by Substack under Substack's privacy policy; we see it only inside our Substack dashboard and use it for nothing except sending the newsletter. Every email has one-click unsubscribe.

What we never do

  • We never sell, rent, or share personal information for advertising.
  • We never run third-party ads or ad trackers.
  • We never receive the data you put into a Tynkr app — by design, we can't.

Children

This site and the Tynkr apps are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Changes

If this policy changes, the new version will be posted at this address with a new effective date. Because we collect so little, changes should be rare and boring.
