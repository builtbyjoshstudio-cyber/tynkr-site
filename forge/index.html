How your data lives.

Open formats

Forge writes one flat JSON file per module — invoices, contacts, purchase orders, contracts, expenses, proposals — plus a receipts folder of ordinary images. Your business records are yours, greppable, and portable.

No proprietary database. No export fee. No account standing between you and your own files. If Forge disappeared tomorrow, this folder still works.

Fair questions.

FAQ
Can Forge send invoices by email?

Forge generates the invoice as a PDF you send however you already email — it doesn’t send mail itself or route your client data through any server.

Where does my business data live?

In flat JSON files on your disk — one per module — plus a receipts folder of ordinary images. Back it up with whatever you already use.

Can multiple people use it?

Forge is built for solo owners and works on one machine at a time. Because the data is flat files, you can share the folder between machines with your own sync — but there’s no multi-user server, by design.

Is there a subscription?

No. Forge is a one-time purchase when it launches. Bug fixes are always free; major version upgrades are a separate purchase. No account, no recurring billing.

Does it work offline?

Yes, completely. The only network request Forge can make is an optional check for updates, which you can turn off.
