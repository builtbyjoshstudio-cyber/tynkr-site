01

The cloud is just someone else's computer. Keep yours.

Why local-first

Every Tynkr app reads and writes plain files in a folder you choose. Open them in any text editor. Sync them however you like — or don't. When we shut down someday, your software keeps working.

No servers

Nothing leaves your machine

No telemetry, no analytics, no accounts. The only network request any Tynkr app ever makes is an optional check for updates — and you can turn that off. Airplane mode is a supported configuration.

Open formats

Files you can actually read

Your ledger is JSON. Your notes are Markdown. No proprietary database, no export hostage-taking. Grep it, script it, back it up with anything.

No rent

Buy once. Own forever.

One-time purchase per app. No monthly toll, no account to create, no license server to phone home. Software the way it used to work.
